Tarvittavan lasim‰‰r‰n tilavuus on helpointa mitata veden avulla. T‰yt‰ mitta-astia vedell‰ ja merkitse astian kylkeen veden korkeus. Mittaa pois haluttu tilavuusm‰‰r‰ vett‰. Measuring the Volume

The volume of glass is easy to measure by using water. Fill a neqasuring bucket with water, and mark the surface level. Take away the wanted volume of eater witha measuring cup. Fill the bucket with glass until the surface is at marked level again.
Measuring the volume is more precise than neasuring by weight.
