Lauri Levanto,

s 27.08.1936 Helsingissä.

Art studies

After graduation, I studued drawing in Highschoool of Arts and Crafts ( now Aalto University) and also in Helsinki University.
Only after relocating into Halikko 1984 my interest was re-activated.
Working with glass I begun 2001.
  • Since 1993 Drawing and sculpting at Salo and Halikko Adult Education Center (Kansalaisopisto)
  • 1997-1999 A two years advanced course in Adult Education Center
  • 2000-2001 KiDe Project for advanced artists, organized by EU Social Fond, Turku University and SAlo Art Museum
  • 2003 Kilnforming Glass at Kankaanpää Institute
  • 2005 Formgivning och Glas, V'ästra Nylands Folkhögskola
  • 2007 Glass Fusing Varnia Oy Suomusjärvi
  • 2002 - 2008 Teaching drawing at Salo Adult Education Center
  • 2004 - 2008 Teaching Glass fusingat Salo Adult Education Center
